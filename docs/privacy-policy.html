Privacy Policy

Ollama 4 Android — Last updated: April 14, 2026

Overview

Ollama 4 Android is a fully offline, on-device AI chat application. Your privacy is fundamental to how this app is designed. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

This app collects no data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports sent externally
  • No cookies or tracking identifiers
  • No advertising or ad-related data
  • No location data

On-Device Processing

All AI inference runs entirely on your device using llama.cpp. Your conversations are processed locally and never leave your phone. Chat history is stored only in a local database on your device.

Internet Usage

The app uses an internet connection only to download AI model files from Hugging Face when you explicitly request a download. After models are downloaded, the app functions completely offline. No conversation data is ever transmitted over the network.

Local API Server

The optional local API server runs on localhost (127.0.0.1) only. It is not accessible from outside your device or over any network. It exists solely to allow other apps on your device to use the loaded AI model.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service accessed is Hugging Face (huggingface.co) for model downloads, subject to their own privacy policy.

Data Storage

All data (downloaded models, chat history, settings) is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. Uninstalling the app removes all data.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
